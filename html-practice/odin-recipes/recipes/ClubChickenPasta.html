Club Chicken Pasta

Home

Description

This chicken pasta salad with crispy bacon, tomatoes, and lots of cooked chicken has all the flavors of a chicken club sandwich. Delicious!

Ingredients
  1. 8 ounces corkscrew-shaped pasta
  2. ¾ cup Italian-style salad dressing or to taste
  3. ¼ cup mayonnaise
  4. 2 cups chopped, cooked rotisserie chicken
  5. 12 slices crispy cooked bacon, crumbled
  6. 8 ounces cherry tomatoes, halved
  7. 1 cup cubed Muenster cheese
  8. 1 cup chopped celery
  9. 1 cup chopped green bell pepper
  10. 1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and chopped
